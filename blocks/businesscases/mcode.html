Applications of Sorting and Pathfinding Algorithms

Environment sustainability

In environmental sustainability, the main problem faced is waste management system. To resolve that and efficiently collect and transport the waste, we use Dijkstra’s Algorithm, as it is used to find the shortest path, like determining the most efficient route for delivery trucks or public transportation systems. This helps in reducing fuel consumption and carbon emissions.

Educational sector

In the education sector, Merge Sort is used to organize student marks efficiently and rank schools or colleges based on average performance.
It is used because Merge Sort can handle large datasets efficiently. In educational systems with thousands of students, Merge Sort ensures that sorting operations remain manageable even as the number of students increases.

If we have 10 students with marks [82, 55, 90, 74, 63, 56, 92, 79, 68, 80], Merge Sort would sort them in ascending order so that we can easily rank the students from lowest to highest marks.

Industrial Strategies

In industrial strategies, companies often have to deal with costs, pricing models, and transportation routes that might involve negative values in this cases Bellman-Fordis used in cases where some paths might have negative costs . It can handle negative weights, unlike Dijkstra’s, which can fail in such situations.. In industries, there’s often a need to prioritize tasks .Quick Sort is used to sort tasks or resources based on priority levels
