Business Cases with SDGs

Industrial Strategies

SDG Target 9.4

Focus: Upgrade infrastructure and retrofit industries to make them sustainable, with increased resource-use efficiency and greater adoption of clean and environmentally sound technologies.

SDG Indicator 9.4.1: CO2 emissions per unit of value added.

SDG Target 12.5

Focus: Substantially reduce waste generation through prevention, reduction, recycling, and reuse.

SDG Indicator 12.5.1: National recycling rate, tons of material recycled.

SDG Target 13.2

Focus: Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies, and planning.

SDG Indicator 13.2.1: Number of countries with integrated climate change policies.

Environmental Sustainability

SDG Target 12.2

Focus: Achieve sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources.

SDG Indicator 12.2.1: Material footprint, material footprint per capita, and material footprint per GDP.

SDG Target 13.1

Focus: Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards and natural disasters in all countries.

SDG Indicator 13.1.1: Number of deaths, missing persons, and directly affected persons attributed to disasters per 100,000 population.

SDG Target 14.1

Focus: Prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds, particularly from land-based activities.

SDG Indicator 14.1.1: Index of coastal eutrophication and floating plastic debris density.

Education Sector

SDG Target 4.1

Focus: Ensure that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults, both men and women, achieve literacy and numeracy.

SDG Indicator 4.1.1: Proportion of youth and adults who have achieved at least a minimum level of proficiency in literacy and numeracy, by sex.

SDG Target 4.4

Focus: Increase the number of youth and adults who have relevant skills for employment, decent jobs, and entrepreneurship.

SDG Indicator 4.4.1: Proportion of youth and adults with ICT skills, by sex.
